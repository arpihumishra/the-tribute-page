Biography

https://www.youtube.com
  • APJ Abdul Kalam served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Kalam was a renowned scientist and engineer who played a key role in India's missile development program, earning him the nickname "Missile Man".
  • Kalam was a prolific author who wrote several books, including "Wings of Fire" and "Ignited Minds". He was also a highly respected inspirational figure, especially among young people.
  • Kalam made significant contributions to India's space research program, including the development of the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV).
  • Kalam was born in a poor Tamil Muslim family in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and sold newspapers as a child to support his family. He went on to study physics and aerospace engineering, and eventually became one of India's most celebrated scientists and leaders.
